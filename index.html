No. The program includes effective bodyweight workouts that you can do at home. If you have weights, even better, but they are not essential to get started.

No, sustainable fat loss allows for flexibility. The 80/20 approach suggests making nutritious choices 80% of the time while allowing 20% for social situations and treats.

Yes, it is safe and designed for all ages. The recommendations are based on international scientific research. We always recommend, as specified in the book, that you consult your doctor before starting any new program.

Less than you think. Workouts lasting 20-45 minutes (3-5 times/week) and simple dietary rules that fit into your life. It's designed to be sustainable.

Not at all! The emphasis on protein and fiber keeps you feeling full and satisfied. In fact, most people report increased energy levels.

The main goal is body recomposition (losing fat and maintaining muscle). The number on the scale may not change dramatically, but your pants will be much looser. Measure your waist!
